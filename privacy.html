Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Holiday Glow Pros (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website, request a free estimate, or communicate with us via SMS/text message.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is completely secure. By using our website, you acknowledge that transmissions may involve risks.

6. Your Rights
